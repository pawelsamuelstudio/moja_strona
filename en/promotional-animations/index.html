Advertising animations for promotion, sales and brand communication

I create promotional animations that help brands present a product, service or idea in an aesthetic, dynamic and modern way.

This type of content works especially well in advertising campaigns, on websites, across social media, in sales presentations and in online communication.

These can take the form of short promo videos, product animations, social media ads, motion design sequences or visual intros for campaigns and branded materials.

I work with clients from Poland and abroad.

This service is a good fit for:

  • promotional campaigns
  • online advertising videos
  • product and service launches
  • social media animations
  • brand presentations
  • promo videos for e-commerce
  • landing pages and offer pages
  • visual brand communication

FAQ

Who is this service for?

This service is a good fit for brands, manufacturers, online stores, agencies and companies that want to promote a product, service or brand through dynamic animation.

What kind of materials can you create?

I can create promotional animations, advertising videos, product animations, motion design, social media content and short videos supporting marketing campaigns.

Do I need to have a finished script?

No. You can come with a finished idea or just the goal of the campaign. I can help organise the concept and choose the right animation format.

How much does a project cost?

The cost depends on the length of the material, number of scenes, level of animation complexity and visual style. Every project is priced individually.

How long does production take?

The timeline depends on the project scope and number of elements to be prepared, but I always define the schedule clearly before starting.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. I work with clients from Poland and abroad, including English-language projects.
