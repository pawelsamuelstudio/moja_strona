Hello and welcome

My name is Paweł Pauksztełło, and I am a motion designer from Poland. I create animations that help businesses, organizations, and individuals communicate their messages clearly and effectively. Whether you need to explain a concept, promote an event, or tell a story, I deliver high-quality animations that engage and inform.

Many people struggle to present their ideas in a way that captures attention and makes an impact. Animation solves this problem by turning information into a visual experience that is easy to understand and remember. I focus on creating animations that support my clients' goals—whether in education, marketing, or storytelling. Portfolio


I also specialize in visualizing Christian themes, helping churches and faith-based organizations share their messages in a meaningful way. Christian Animation
