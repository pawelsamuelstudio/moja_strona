The Gospel Explained in 5 Minutes

Project: animated Bible video / Collaboration: Bible Unbound / Script: Austin Smith

The Gospel Explained in Five Minutes is a fully animated interpretation of the Gospel created in collaboration with Bible Unbound and Austin Smith, who wrote the script and handled the sound design, while I focused on the illustrations and animation.

The film presents the Gospel in the context of the entire Bible, highlighting key moments from Scripture and connecting Old Testament events with the New Testament.

The project was both challenging and rewarding, exploring how biblical animation can serve as a tool for education and spiritual growth.

This animation offers an accessible way to understand the Gospel and God's plan of salvation.
