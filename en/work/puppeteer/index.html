Puppeteer - Animated Music Video

Project: animated music video / Technique: 2D and 3D animation / Client: YAGOOSH

About the project:

“Puppeteer” is an animated music video created at the request of Agnieszka Majcher from the band YAGOOSH. The project combines 2D and 3D animation, creating a poetic story about emotions, relationships, and human fragility.

The inspiration came from the photographs of Magdalena Szczykutowicz, which I brought to life through subtle 2D animation and enriched with 3D elements, giving them a new symbolic dimension. The characters move like puppets guided by invisible forces — a metaphor for dependence and inner searching.

Additional effects — floating clouds, twinkling lights, and abstract shapes — create an atmosphere of nostalgia and reflection, harmonizing with the emotional tone of the band’s music.

“Puppeteer” is not only an animated music video but also a visual journey through the world of feelings and thoughts, encouraging reflection on relationships and human nature.
