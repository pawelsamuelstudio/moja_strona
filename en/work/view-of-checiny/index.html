View of Checiny - Animated Painting

Project: animated painting / Client: National Museum in Kielce / Original artwork: Józef Szermentowski

Józef Szermentowski’s "View of Checiny" has been reimagined through digital animation, transporting audiences to 19th-century Checiny by blending classical painting with modern animation techniques.

The project allows viewers to explore the historical details of the artwork and better understand the context in which it was created, while highlighting the richness of 19th-century painting.

The National Museum in Kielce presents Szermentowski and Kostrzewski in a dynamic and engaging form, attracting a wide audience and offering a fresh perspective on historical art.

This unique experience combines history, culture, and technology, showing how traditional painting can come to life in motion and space.
