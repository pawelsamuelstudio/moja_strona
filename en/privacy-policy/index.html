Privacy Policy

1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, and protected on the website samuelstudio.com, operated by Paweł Pauksztełło (Samuel Studio).

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which provides the technical infrastructure for displaying this site. GitHub may collect basic technical information, such as your IP address or browser type, solely for the purpose of ensuring proper website performance and security. For details, please see GitHub’s Privacy Statement.

2. Data Collected

This website does not collect personal data automatically and does not use cookies, analytics tools, or contact forms. You can visit and browse this site freely without providing any personal information.

Personal data is collected only if you choose to contact me directly via e-mail at pawel.samuelstudio@gmail.com. In that case, I will process your data (such as your name, e-mail address, and message content) solely for the purpose of replying to your inquiry.

3. Purpose and Legal Basis of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed exclusively to:

  • respond to your messages or project inquiries,
  • maintain necessary correspondence.

The legal basis for processing your data is Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR — legitimate interest consisting of maintaining communication related to my professional activity.

4. Data Retention

Your personal data will be stored only as long as necessary to handle the correspondence or fulfill any resulting cooperation. After that, the data will be deleted, unless further storage is required by law.

5. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be shared, sold, or transferred to third parties, except when required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights

According to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • access your data,
  • correct or delete it,
  • restrict processing,
  • object to processing,
  • lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority (President of the Personal Data Protection Office in Poland).

To exercise your rights, please contact me by e-mail: pawel.samuelstudio@gmail.com

7. External Links

This website may contain links to other websites (for example social media profiles, YouTube, or Behance). I am not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect legal or technical changes. The current version is always available on the Samuel Studio website.
