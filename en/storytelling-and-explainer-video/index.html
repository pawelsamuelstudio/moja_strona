Storytelling and explainer video for websites, campaigns and brand communication

I create explainer videos and storytelling-based visuals that help present a product, service or idea in a clear, engaging and modern way.

This type of video works especially well when you want to explain how something works, show a problem and solution, simplify a complex message or make your brand easier to understand.

FAQ

Who is this service for?

This service is a good fit for businesses, brands, startups, agencies and anyone who wants to explain an offer or product in a clear and engaging way.

What can an explainer video show?

It can explain a product, service, process, app or business model, or simply present the value of your offer in a clear and engaging format.

Can you also help with the message itself?

Yes. I can help structure the content, simplify the message and turn it into a more effective visual narrative.

Is this type of video suitable for a landing page?

Yes. Explainer videos work very well on landing pages because they help visitors understand the offer faster and can support conversion.

How much does a project cost?

The price depends on the video length, complexity, visual style, number of scenes and the amount of concept work involved. Each project is priced individually.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. I work with clients from Poland and abroad, including English-language projects.
