Logo animation for advertising, digital content and brand presentation

I create animated logos that help brands look modern, professional and memorable. It works especially well wherever a static logo is not enough and motion can add energy, clarity and a stronger first impression.

Logo animation can be used on websites, in video ads, promotional materials, social media, intros, presentations, reels and branding campaigns.

Depending on your brand style, I can create subtle logo motion, dynamic logo intros, elegant premium animation or more expressive 2D and 3D logo sequences tailored to your visual communication.

FAQ

Who is this service for?

This service works well for personal brands, companies, startups, agencies, manufacturers and any business that wants to present its logo professionally in motion.

What kinds of logo animation can you create?

I can create subtle logo motion, intro and outro animation, branding sequences, 2D logo animation, 3D logo animation and short motion pieces for advertising and social media.

Do I need to have my logo in vector format?

It is best if you have your logo in vector format, but if you do not, send me what you have and we can check what is possible to work with.

What formats will I receive?

I can prepare the final animation in formats suitable for websites, social media, video editing, presentations or other marketing use.

How much does a project cost?

The price depends on the animation style, length, complexity and number of versions. Every project is quoted individually.

How long does it take?

The timeline depends on the scope of the project and the number of versions, but I always define it clearly before starting the work.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. I work with clients worldwide, including English-language projects.
