View of Checiny Animation: Merging Traditional Art with Digital Technology


Józef Szermentowski's "View of Checiny" has been reimagined through digital animation, transporting audiences to 19th-century Checiny by combining classical painting with modern animation techniques. The project allows viewers to explore the historical details of the painting and understand the context in which it was created while highlighting the richness of 19th-century art. The National Museum in Kielce presents Szermentowski and Kostrzewski in a dynamic, engaging format, attracting a wide audience. This unique experience blends history, culture, and technology, showing how traditional painting can come to life in motion and space.
