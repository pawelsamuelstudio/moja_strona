Animated Gospel – The Gospel Explained in Five Minutes


The Gospel Explained in Five Minutes is a fully animated interpretation of the Gospel created with Bible Unbound and Austin Smith, who wrote the script and handled sound design, while I focused on illustrations and animation. The film presents the Gospel in the context of the entire Bible, highlighting key moments from Scripture and connecting Old Testament events to the New Testament. The project was both challenging and rewarding, exploring how biblical animation can serve as a tool for education and spiritual growth. This animation offers an accessible way to understand the Gospel and God's plan of salvation.
