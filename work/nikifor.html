NIKIFOR - Motion Design for National Museum in Kielce

Nikifor Lemko, also known as Epifaniusz Drowniak, created extraordinary works using humble materials such as paper, cardboard, and cheap paints. His paintings, watercolors, and drawings remain a source of inspiration for artists worldwide.

For the National Museum in Kielce, I produced a short promotional motion graphic for the exhibition at the Museum of Dialogue Between Cultures. Using Nikifor's self-portrait, I created a simple character rig animation and incorporated elements from his paintings along with cardboard-style backgrounds. This approach highlighted the artist's unique style while enhancing the exhibition's visual identity. The video offers viewers an engaging way to explore Nikifor's life, artistic development, and the region that inspired him.
