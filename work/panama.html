Panama in Kielce - Animated Promotional Film

Project: animated promotional film / Topic: World Youth Day / Location: Kielce

Creating an animated film for Panama in Kielce for people who could not attend World Youth Day in Panama was a unique creative challenge.

Inspired by the official event logo, the project was designed to capture the energy and spirit of this global celebration. The animation aimed to engage young audiences and bring the atmosphere of Panama to life, even through a screen.

While nothing can fully replace being there in person, the film helped connect participants in Kielce with the excitement of World Youth Day.

Through dynamic visuals and engaging storytelling, it celebrated joy, community, and enthusiasm, showing that the spirit of Panama can be experienced far beyond its borders.
