PANAMA IN KIELCE



Creating an animated film to showcase Panama in Kielce for those unable to attend World Youth Day in Panama was a unique adventure. Inspired by the official event logo, the project aimed to capture the energy and spirit of this global celebration. The animation was designed to engage young audiences and bring the vibrant streets and atmosphere of Panama to life, even through a screen. While nothing can fully replace being in Panama, the film helped connect participants in Kielce with the excitement of World Youth Day. Through dynamic visuals and captivating storytelling, it celebrated community and enthusiasm, showing that the spirit of Panama lives on far beyond its borders.
