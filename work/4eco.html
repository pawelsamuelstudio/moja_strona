A Small Story About Big Energy


I had the opportunity to create a vibrant 2D animation from scratch for 4ECO, a company demonstrating that ecology, cost efficiency, and modern energy solutions can work in harmony.

The animation brings movement and color to the story of how heat pumps and energy efficiency power panels, impacting everyday life while contributing to a sustainable future.

Creating this project from the ground up was a rewarding experience, allowing me to combine creativity, design, and technology to convey meaningful values.

I'm proud to have contributed to a project that encourages smart, eco-friendly decisions and inspires action toward a greener future.
