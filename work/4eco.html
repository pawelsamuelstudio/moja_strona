4ECO 2D Animation

Project: 2D animation / Client: 4ECO / Topic: photovoltaics, heat pumps and renewable energy solutions

I had the opportunity to create a vibrant 2D animation from scratch for 4ECO, a company demonstrating that ecology, cost efficiency, and modern energy solutions can work in harmony.

The animation brings movement and color to the story of how photovoltaics, heat pumps, and energy efficiency influence everyday life while contributing to a more sustainable future.

Creating this project from the ground up was a rewarding experience, allowing me to combine creativity, design, and technology to communicate meaningful values.

I am proud to have contributed to a project that encourages smart, eco-friendly decisions and inspires action toward a greener future.
