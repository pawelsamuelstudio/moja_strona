KOSMOS – Animated Collage Film

Project: experimental animation / Technique: animated collage / Theme: urban space and city iconography

Animated collages reveal the visual iconography of the street. These fluid and creative compositions capture the complexity of the urban landscape while conveying the emotions and atmosphere of city life.

Through experimental animation techniques, the film transforms fragments of urban imagery into a dynamic visual narrative that surprises and engages the viewer.
