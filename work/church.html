Church of the Holy Spirit

I invite you to journey through 35 years of my parish’s history. My animated film presents it as a series of turning pages, each revealing a new stage — from the earliest days of the community, through successive churches, to the creation of a magnificent new sanctuary.

With 3D technology and a virtual book, the story becomes vivid and tangible. Each page uncovers the people who passionately built the community, the joyful moments, and the challenges that shaped the parish’s spiritual path.

Immerse yourself in this extraordinary journey and discover the history of the Church of the Holy Spirit from a completely new perspective.
