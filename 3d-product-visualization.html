FAQ

Who is this service for?

This service is a good fit for brands, manufacturers, online stores, agencies and companies that want to present products in an attractive way.

What kind of 3D product visuals can you create?

I can create CGI packshots, product animations, commercial renders, short promotional videos and visual presentations showing product features.

Do I need to have a ready 3D model?

Not always. If you already have a model, I can work with it. If not, the scope can be discussed individually depending on the product and source materials available.

How much does a project cost?

The price depends on product complexity, animation length, number of shots and visual style. Every project is priced individually.

How long does production take?

The timeline depends on the scope of the project, but it is always discussed clearly before production begins.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. I work with clients from Poland and abroad, including English-speaking projects.
